SheCodes

SheCodes is an online coding platform designed to empower women by teaching them programming skills in a supportive environment. It offers structured workshops on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web development tools, enabling beginners to build real-world projects. With a focus on bridging the gender gap in tech, SheCodes provides hands-on learning, mentorship, and community support. The platform also offers certifications and career guidance, helping women advance in the tech industry.
